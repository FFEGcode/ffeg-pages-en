The planning pathway

Where does the Foundation Course fit?

The FFEG planning pathway has four levels. The Foundation Course builds the planning and periodisation knowledge that underpins development at every level.

01

Emergent

Planning is reactive and largely session-to-session.

02

Developing

A recognisable structure exists, but consistency varies.

03

Structured

Planning is deliberate and consistently applied across sessions, weeks and blocks.

04

Systematic

Planning is fully integrated and applied with precision across all dimensions.

Wherever you are on the pathway, the Foundation Course gives you a clear framework for the decisions you make every week.

What you will learn

A framework for effective planning.

  • 1 You'll understand why periodisation models exist, what problems they are designed to solve, and when each is appropriate in your environment.
  • 2 You'll learn how to structure a training week that supports player availability, performance readiness, and physical preparation.
  • 3 You'll begin to build a planning framework you can apply in your own environment.
